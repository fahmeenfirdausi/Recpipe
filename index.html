White Chocolate Cinnimon Chip Bars

Recpie By Chef Hiba Kanwal



Ingredients


  • Butter 1/2 cup (unsalted, melted)
  • Egg 1 large
  • Light brown sugar 1 cup packed
  • Vanilla essence 2 tsp
  • Cinnimon 2 tsp
  • All purpose flour 1 cup
  • Cinnimon chips 1/2 cups
  • White chocolate 8 ounces (melted)

Cooking Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Line an 8-by-8-inch baking pan with aluminum foil, spray with cooking spray; set aside.
  3. Melt the butter in a microwave bowl about 1 minute on high power.
  4. Let wait to lower down the temperature then add egg, brown sugar, vanilla essence, cinnamon and all purpose flour.
  5. Mix all nicely until well combined.
  6. Mix 1 ¼ cups cinnamon chips in batter.
  7. Pour batter in prepared baking pan. Level the top with a spatula.
  8. Bake for about 18 to 20 minutes, or until done.
  9. Insert a toothpick in the center if it should come out clean, it is done. Allow bars to cool.
  10. For topping melt the white chocolate in a microwave save bowl.
  11. Pour melted chocolate over the bars in an even layer and level it.
  12. Sprinkle with ¼ cup cinnamon chips.
  13. Allow white chocolate to cool completely then slice and serve.
